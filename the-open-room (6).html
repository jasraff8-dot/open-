About The Open Room

The Open Room was founded on a single conviction: that great architecture and great education are inseparable. We are a practice that designs buildings — and a school that designs architects.

Our Mission

To practice architecture that is honest, contextual, and deeply human — while building an educational environment where the next generation of designers discovers their voice, their vision, and their discipline.

Our Vision

To become Tamil Nadu's most trusted convergence of architectural practice and design education — a place where the studio and the classroom speak to each other, constantly, productively, and beautifully.

Our Values

Integrity in practice. Rigour in education. Humility in design. We believe every space — however small — deserves thoughtful attention, and every student — however early — deserves honest mentorship.

Our Journey

HOW WE
GOT HERE

2022
The Idea Takes Root

Principal Architect Javid Asraff S begins developing a concept — a firm where active design practice informs teaching, and teaching sharpens practice. Early sketches, conversations, and collaborations begin.

2023
First Student Batch — NATA Coaching

Operating from a small studio space in Coimbatore, The Open Room conducts its first NATA preparation batch. 12 students. 11 selections. The model is validated.

2024
The Firm is Officially Founded

The Open Room is formally registered as an architectural practice and educational institution. The studio takes its first commercial commissions while expanding NATA and B.Arch programs simultaneously.

2025
Academic Collaborations Launched

Formal partnerships with architecture colleges across Tamil Nadu are established. Joint studios, guest critique programs, and internship pipelines connect The Open Room to the broader academic community.

Now
Expanding. Deepening. Building.

With a growing portfolio of projects and a thriving educational wing, The Open Room continues to grow — not just in scale, but in depth of impact on students, clients, and communities.

NATA PREPARATION PROGRAM

Our NATA coaching program is built by practicing architects who understand what examiners look for. We combine drawing practice, aptitude training, and design thinking in an intensive curriculum that consistently produces top results.

  • Comprehensive drawing & sketching modules
  • Mathematics & aptitude crash courses
  • Mock tests modeled on actual NATA pattern
  • One-on-one portfolio review sessions
  • Live studio projects alongside practicing architects
  • Interview preparation for top B.Arch colleges
  • Small batch sizes — maximum attention per student
